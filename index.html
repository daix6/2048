WHAT'S THIS: An reimplementation of 2048 by Shawn.
HOW TO PLAY: In PC, use W, A, S, D or ↑, ←, ↓, → to move these pawns. Use your fingers instead in mobile devices.
RULES: Pawns with same numbers will merge together.
If the biggest number in the grid comes up to 2048, you win!
If you can not move these pawns any more and you don't get 2048, game ends.
